RADAR CHART

The Radar Chart in the RIASEC test is a visual tool used to represent an individual's interests in different job-related categories. It displays preferences across six key areas: Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, and Conventional (RIASEC). Each area forms a point on the chart, and the distance from the center to the point indicates the level of interest. This tool aids in identifying potential career paths based on these preferences.
