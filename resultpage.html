RADAR CHART 

The Radar Chart in the RIASEC test is a visual tool used to represent an individual's interests in different job-related categories. It displays your preferences across six key areas: Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, and Conventional (RIASEC). Each area forms a point on the chart, and the distance from the center to the point indicates the level of interest in that category. It helps identify your preferences and potential career paths based on those preferences.
